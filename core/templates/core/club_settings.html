Operating hours

The full span of the daily calendar. Aircraft and bookings outside these hours are not displayed.

Booking slots

The standard time slots members can book into. These appear in the mobile booking finder and define the “normal” flying window on the calendar — anything within operating hours but outside these slots is shown as atypical and requires the member to confirm before saving. Add as many as you like, in any order. Skip lunch, mix durations, add evening slots.

Booking blocks

Prevent members from making new bookings when account conditions aren't met. Instructors and admins are always exempt.
