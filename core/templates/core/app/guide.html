Welcome 👋

This is your club app for {{ club.name }}. Here's a quick tour of what each part does.

🏠 The five tabs

  • Home — your next flights, notices, and quick actions.
  • Schedule — the day's bookings across all aircraft and instructors.
  • Bookings — your own bookings, past and upcoming.
  • Account — your balance and payment history.
  • Profile — your details, credentials, and settings.

✈️ Booking a flight

1Tap Book and choose what you want — aircraft, and an instructor if you need one.
2Pick from the times that are free, then confirm your request.
3Your booking starts as Pending. You'll get a notification when the club confirms it.
4On the day, an instructor or duty member checks you out and back in. The cost is added to your account automatically.
Can't find a slot? The app only shows times that are actually free for that aircraft and instructor. Try a different time or aircraft.

🪪 Your credentials

Your medical, flight review (BFR), and ratings decide what you can book and fly. Keep them current under Profile → Add credential.

If a credential lapses you may be blocked from booking or checking out until it's renewed and recorded. Add the new expiry date as soon as you have it.

💳 Account & invoices

Your Account tab shows your running balance — flights are charged to it, payments credit it. When the club issues you an invoice (like your annual subscription), you'll be notified. Pay it the way your club normally accepts; the office marks it paid once received.

🔔 Notifications

Turn on notifications under Profile → Notifications to hear about confirmed bookings, new invoices, and club notices. You can add this app to your phone's home screen from your browser's Share → Add to Home Screen menu.

📋 Training notes

After a dual lesson, your instructor may write a debrief note covering what you practised, feedback on the flight, and a plan for your next lesson. These are saved to your record permanently.

Find them under Profile → Training notes. Your instructor may also email a copy directly to you — you'll receive it at your registered email address.

❓ Need a hand?

If a charge looks wrong, a booking didn't confirm, or you can't sign in, contact the club office or your instructor — they can see your account and bookings and help sort it out.
